Why I do not identify as a feminist

Have I been cancelled yet?

When someone says the word "feminist" today, does someone seeking equality come to mind? Maybe. Maybe not.
Many people visualise a feminist as a lady who comes and screams in your face about how trash you are. Feminism has gained
a connotation of hating men. People can blabber all they want about how the original goal of feminism was to spread equality
but it's reputation was destroyed by many people who associate themselves with it.

I like to compare it to this: Imagine going out into the street and telling everyone that you are gay. Will they assume that
you mean you are happy (the original meaning)? No. They will assume that you mean you are homosexual.

In the same way, the word "feminism" has gained a connotation that no longer allows it to mean equality. It just
seems to be about how women are better than men.

So I am all for equality. I just don't call myself a feminist because of how the word is viewed in modern times.
